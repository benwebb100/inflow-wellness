Wellness eGuides

Practical, downloadable guides designed to support your wellness journey between sessions and beyond.

Written by Keren and grounded in evidence-based advice, each guide covers a specific area of movement, nutrition or healthy living — giving you clear, actionable steps you can apply right away.

Why eGuides?

These guides are designed to extend the support you receive in sessions into your everyday life. Each one distils practical advice into a clear, easy-to-follow format — so you can continue making progress on your own terms.

Whether you're looking for meal ideas, movement guidance or strategies for building healthier habits, there's a guide to help you along the way.
