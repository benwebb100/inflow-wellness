Meet Keren

Physiotherapist. Personal Trainer. Pilates Instructor. Integrative Health Coach. Travel Agent.

With a unique combination of qualifications across movement, health and lifestyle, Keren helps women build strength, confidence and long-term wellbeing through a supportive, practical and holistic approach.

Her goal is simple — to help women feel strong, capable and able to enjoy life fully, both now and in the years ahead.

Philosophy

Health should enhance the way you live — not dominate it. Keren believes that caring for your body can be positive, enjoyable and fully integrated into real life, rather than something that requires extreme discipline or restriction.

Her philosophy centres on practical, sustainable health. Rather than short-term programs or quick fixes, the focus is on building strength gradually, developing nourishing habits and creating a lifestyle that supports how you want to feel — not just how you want to look.

This means working with each client as an individual, understanding her goals, her challenges and her life — and building a plan that genuinely fits.

My Story

Keren's journey into wellness began through physiotherapy — helping people recover, rebuild and reconnect with their bodies. Over time, she recognised that true wellbeing goes beyond rehabilitation. It requires movement, nourishment and a lifestyle that supports the whole person.

This led her to expand her expertise across personal training, Pilates, integrative health coaching and even travel planning — because sometimes the most powerful step towards health is stepping away from everyday life and experiencing something new.

Today, InFlow Wellness brings all of these elements together in one supportive, integrated approach.

My Approach

Every client is different. Keren's approach is grounded in understanding where you are, where you want to be and what will realistically work for your life.

Supportive, Not Prescriptive

Guidance that reassures and encourages — never intense or overwhelming. You'll feel safe to ask questions, move at your pace and build confidence gradually.

Practical and Realistic

Programs and plans that fit into your actual life — not an idealised version of it. Sustainable habits that you can maintain long-term.

Holistic and Integrated

Movement, nutrition and lifestyle work together. Keren's diverse qualifications mean she can support you across multiple dimensions of health.

Premium yet Approachable

Expert-level guidance delivered with warmth and humanity. You'll feel supported by someone who genuinely cares about your progress and wellbeing.
