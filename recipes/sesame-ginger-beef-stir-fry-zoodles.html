Sesame Ginger Beef Stir Fry with Zoodles

Serves: 4 Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp tamari
  • 1 tbsp xylitol (or other sweetener)
  • 2 tbsp rice wine vinegar
  • 450g ground lean beef (5% fat)
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp fresh ginger, grated
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 medium zucchinis, spiralized

Instructions

  1. 1Combine tamari, rice wine vinegar, and sweetener in a bowl. Set aside.
  2. 2Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat. Cook beef for 7–10 minutes until browned.
  3. 3Push beef to one side of the pan. Add sesame oil, ginger, and garlic to the empty space and cook for 1 minute.
  4. 4Pour in the sauce and stir to coat the beef. Cook for 1 minute.
  5. 5Briefly heat spiralized zucchini with the beef before serving.

Nutrition Per Serve

Energy

382 kcal

Carbs

5g

Protein

23g

Fat

30g
