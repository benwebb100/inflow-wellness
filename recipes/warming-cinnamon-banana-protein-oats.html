Warming Cinnamon Banana Protein Oats

Servings: 1

Ingredients

  • ½ cup sprouted rolled oats
  • ¾ cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 scoop vanilla plant-based or whey protein powder
  • 1 medium banana, half sliced for topping
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon

Instructions

  1. 1.In a small pot, combine oats and almond milk. Cook over medium heat for 3–5 minutes, stirring often, until thick and creamy.
  2. 2.Remove from heat and stir in protein powder and cinnamon until fully blended.
  3. 3.Pour into a bowl, top with banana slices and an extra splash of milk if desired.
  4. 4.Sprinkle with an extra pinch of cinnamon and serve warm.

Protein

21g per serving

Prep Time

5 minutes

Cook Time

10 minutes

Nutrition note: Excellent source of Protein, Fiber, Vitamin B6, Manganese, Magnesium, and Potassium.
