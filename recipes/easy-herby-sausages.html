Easy Herby Sausages

Serves: 8 Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 250g ground beef
  • 2 tbsp chopped sage
  • 2 tbsp chopped basil
  • 1 tbsp coconut oil

Instructions

  1. 1.Mix the ground beef with salt, pepper, sage, and basil in a bowl. Shape into eight sausages.
  2. 2.Heat coconut oil in a large non-stick pan over medium heat.
  3. 3.Cook the sausages until browned and cooked through.
  4. 4.Serve hot or refrigerate for later.

Nutrition Per Serve

Energy

87 kcal

Carbs

0 g

Protein

6 g

Fat

7 g
