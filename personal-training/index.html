Personal Training

Building strength, confidence and long-term health through structured, well-performed and supportive exercise.

Sessions are tailored specifically towards each client's personal goals. The focus is on practical, functional, effective strength and movement training that helps women feel capable in their bodies whilst maintaining their independence, energy and confidence as they move through different stages of life.

View Session Options

How Sessions Work

Rather than extreme workouts or short-term programs, the focus is on building strength gradually, improving mobility and supporting overall wellbeing so that exercise becomes something that enhances everyday life.

Each session is structured around your individual goals, fitness level and any considerations such as injuries or health conditions. Sessions are available as 1:1 (individual) or 2:1 (pair) formats, in 30-minute or 45-minute durations.

The goal is to help you feel strong, capable and able to enjoy life fully, both now and in the years ahead.

Who This Is For

Personal training at inflow-wellness is designed for women of all levels:

  • Women who are new to exercise or experienced with exercise
  • Those who are returning after time away
  • Those who feel overwhelmed and unsure of what to do in the gym
  • Those who require modifications due to injuries
  • Women navigating midlife and hormonal changes who want a realistic and sustainable way to stay active and feel strong

What You Can Expect

Improved Strength & Muscle Tone

Build functional strength that supports your everyday life and long-term independence.

Greater Confidence in Movement

Feel confident and capable with exercise, knowing your technique is guided and supported.

Improved Energy & Wellbeing

Experience better energy levels, mood and overall sense of vitality through regular movement.

Bone & Metabolic Health

Support your bone density and metabolic health — especially important through midlife and beyond.

Sustainable Approach

A realistic approach to staying active that becomes part of your life, not a burden on it.

Supportive Environment

Guidance, accountability and a genuinely supportive space to build your confidence with movement.
