Lifestyle & Experiences

Helping women live well — not just be well. Support that translates health into everyday energy, balance and enjoyment.

While movement and nutrition form the foundation of health, this pillar focuses on how that health translates into everyday life — including energy, balance, recovery and the ability to fully enjoy meaningful experiences such as travel.

Get in Touch

Creating a Life That Supports Your Health

Support in this area is designed to help women create a lifestyle that feels sustainable, enjoyable and aligned with their goals, rather than something that feels restrictive or overwhelming.

This may include guidance around daily routines, recovery practices and overall wellbeing, alongside access to services such as infrared sauna for relaxation and recovery, and support in planning travel experiences that complement a healthy and balanced lifestyle.

Who This Is For

This pillar is suited to women who want more than just exercise and nutrition and are looking to create a lifestyle that supports how they want to feel day-to-day. It is particularly helpful for women who:

  • Want to feel more energised and balanced in everyday life
  • Are looking to prioritise recovery, rest and overall wellbeing
  • Want guidance on creating sustainable routines that support their health
  • Are interested in incorporating wellness practices such as sauna into their routine
  • Want to travel in a way that feels aligned with their health and lifestyle
  • Value experiences and want to enjoy life fully, not just focus on health alone

Infrared Sauna

The infrared sauna is a simple, supportive addition to your routine — designed to help you slow down, recover and take care of your body in a way that feels sustainable and realistic.

This isn't about extremes or quick fixes. It's about creating space to pause, reset and support your overall wellbeing.

Infrared sauna sessions can assist with:

  • Recovery after training
  • Circulation support
  • Stress reduction
  • Taking time to slow down
  • Building consistent, supportive habits into your week

How It Works

The sauna is available as both an add-on to personal training sessions or as a stand-alone booking.

You can choose a session length that suits your schedule, with options designed to be easy to incorporate into your routine.

Infrared sauna sessions can also be added to any of my existing programs as an additional support option.
